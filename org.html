Descriptions

Case Studies

This section includes some real-world examples of positive organizational implementations of resilience. Case studies include Amazon's Day 1 philosophy; Delta Airlines' 5th SMS policy: Leadership; the Boeing 737 MAX as a case study; Zipline drones demonstrating organizational resilience; and a negative application of not showing resilience--a story about a bus driver who didn't call out, but simple switched jobs.

Org Development

This section includes recommended organization-wide rules, practices and behaviors that promote Human Performance & Resilience in the organization. Some policies include: eliminating jerks; determining and communication norms (the "unspoken" culture); developing a LIT program for personal leadership; and promotion as a positive culture-builder.

Exercises

This section includes exercises that will demonstrate the topics mentioned in the other sections and encourage the students to implement them into their organizations.
